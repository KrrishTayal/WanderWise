# WanderWise - Your Ultimate Travel Companion for Exploring India

Welcome to **WanderWise**, your go-to platform for discovering and planning your perfect trip across India. Whether you are an adventure seeker, a cultural explorer, or a leisure traveler, WanderWise provides you with the right tools to craft personalized itineraries, explore hidden gems, and make the most of your journey.

## Features

### 1. **Custom Itinerary Planner**
   - Create a personalized travel itinerary based on your interests, duration, and budget.
   - Get AI-powered recommendations for destinations, attractions, and activities.
   - Save and share your itinerary with fellow travelers.

### 2. **Comprehensive Destination Guide**
   - Explore detailed information about India’s top tourist destinations, including historical sites, cultural hotspots, and natural wonders.
   - Discover offbeat and lesser-known places to enhance your travel experience.

### 3. **Travel Tools & Resources**
   - Weather forecasts, local transport guides, and accommodation suggestions.
   - Currency converter, language translation, and packing checklists.
   - Safety tips and emergency contact details for travelers.

### 4. **Exclusive Travel Deals & Packages**
   - Get access to exclusive deals on hotels, flights, and local experiences.
   - Book guided tours and activities tailored to your preferences.

### 5. **Community & Reviews**
   - Read reviews and travel experiences shared by fellow WanderWise users.
   - Share your own travel stories, tips, and recommendations.
